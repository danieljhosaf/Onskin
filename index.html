OnSkin leverages a team of expert scientists who meticulously evaluate the ingredients in cosmetic products. The safety scores assigned to these ingredients are based on rigorous scientific research, ensuring that you receive accurate and reliable information.

Tips for Choosing Safe and Effective Skincare Products

  • Look for products labeled as "hypoallergenic" or "fragrance-free."
  • Thoroughly read the ingredient list and avoid those you know you are sensitive to.
  • Conduct a patch test before using a new product.
  • Be cautious of products making unrealistic claims.
  • Consult your doctor or dermatologist for personalized advice.
